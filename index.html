Your guide to nature's best paths

Site Name

Eco Trails Adventure - This name captures the essence of an environmentally focused hiking and outdoor adventure guide. It is memorable, relevant to eco-tourism, and brandable. Optional domain availability: ecotrails.org.

Site Purpose

The website offers a platform for outdoor enthusiasts to discover and book eco-friendly trail experiences, read detailed trail information, and connect with guides and fellow hikers. It promotes responsible travel and sustainability through curated trails and educational content.

Scenarios

  • What are the most beginner-friendly eco-trails in my area?
  • How do I book a guided hike for my group?

Color Schema

  • Forest Green (#2F5233): Used for headers and call-to-action buttons.
  • Earthy Beige (#F5F0E1): Background color for content sections.
  • Sky Blue (#87BFFF): Accent color for links and highlights.

Typography

  • Heading Font: "Roboto Slab", serif – used for all headings and titles.
  • Body Font: "Open Sans", sans-serif – used for paragraphs and UI labels.
