About Springfield Chamber of Commerce

Our History

Founded in 1925, the Springfield Chamber of Commerce has been serving the business community for nearly a century. What began as a small group of local merchants has grown into a powerful organization representing businesses of all sizes across our region.

Through economic booms and challenges, the Chamber has remained a constant advocate for our business community, helping to shape policies and create opportunities that benefit all members.

Our Mission

To foster a thriving business environment that promotes economic growth, advocates for pro-business policies, and provides valuable resources and networking opportunities for our members.
